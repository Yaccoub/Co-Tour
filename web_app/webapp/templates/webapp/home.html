Key Features

CO-TOUR enables users to explores changes in the tourist flow due to the COVID19-crisis in the city of Munich. Using a wide spectrum of data-driven approaches. CO-TOUR presents various models that provide to analyze the tourism flow in the next period and deliver a tourism recommendation system for Munich post-COVID19.

Tourist Flow Analysis

Cluster the attractions in Munich by similarity. Explore the changes in tourism patterns before and after the pandemic.

Tourist Hotspot Forecast

Discover the impact of COVID-19 on the popularity of the different attractions in Munich

Recommendation System

A personalised recommendation system that suggests attractions to visit based on the user entries
